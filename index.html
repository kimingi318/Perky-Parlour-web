About Me

My name is Perpetual, I am a professional makeup artist with over 5 years of experience. I am passionate about my work and I love to make my clients feel beautiful and confident. I specialize in bridal makeup, editorial makeup, and special events. I use high-quality products to ensure that my clients look and feel their best. I am dedicated to providing excellent customer service and I always go above and beyond to make sure that my clients are happy with their makeup. I am committed to staying up-to-date on the latest makeup trends and techniques so that I can provide my clients with the best possible service. I look forward to working with you and helping you look and feel your best!
